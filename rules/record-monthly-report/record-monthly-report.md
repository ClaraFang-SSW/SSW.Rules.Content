To enhance transparency and keep the board informed on key business metrics, each SSW office will produce a short video report each month for the Board Meeting.

### Prepare Content Outline:

#### Create a structured list in SharePoint to cover the following key points:
1. Booked In Days from Power BI
2. Utilization
3. Sales Updates/Opportunities
4. Marketing Plan – key activities and performance metrics.
5. HR Recruitment – updates on hiring progress and open positions.
6. Transfer Payment Report and Invoices – status and summary of payments.
7. Other Finance-Related Metrics – such as:
     - Gas in the Tank
     - Cashflow Summary
     - Profit and Loss Statement

### Record the Video
Include Accountant and State Manager in the recording where possible.

Aim for a concise video under 10 minutes.

### Upload the video to the SSW Finance Youtube Channel 🔐
Use the dedicated [SSW Finance Channel](https://www.youtube.com/@sswfinance) for uploads.

The video must be recorded and published as Unlisted on this channel by the 15th of each month.

Once published, add the video link to the corresponding Board Meeting agenda.
